Welcome to All Star Youth Club

Established in 2074 B.S., All Star Youth Club is a nonprofit organization actively working in the field of sports and youth development in Beldandi-1, Kanchanpur, Nepal.

About Us

All Star Youth Club is dedicated to promoting sports, youth engagement, and community development. We aim to empower young individuals through various programs, events, and social initiatives in our community.
